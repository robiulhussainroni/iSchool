Why Choose Us?

iSchool has achieved the highest success rate among all schools in the region. Our students consistently perform well, guided by dedicated faculty and innovative teaching methods. This accomplishment reflects our commitment to supporting every student's growth and helping them reach their full potential.
iSchool offers a range of additional courses designed to enrich our students' educational experience. From advanced STEM programs to creative arts and language courses, we provide opportunities for students to explore their interests and develop new skills. These extra courses are tailored to support personal growth and prepare students for future challenges in a well-rounded manner.
iSchool fosters a vibrant and supportive environment that enhances the learning experience. Our modern campus features open, well-lit spaces and natural elements, creating a welcoming atmosphere for students. With a focus on collaboration and personal well-being, iSchool provides a nurturing setting where students feel comfortable and motivated to thrive.

About Us..

Founded in 2004 by two friends with a shared vision for innovative education, iSchool has grown into a leading institution dedicated to student success. Our founders' commitment to excellence and personalized learning has shaped iSchool into a nurturing environment where curiosity and growth are fostered. We continue to build on this foundation, providing a supportive and dynamic atmosphere that prepares students.
